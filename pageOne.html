First Writing Post

foot on the gas pedal
you’re putting in the work, the time, the energy
the engine is revving
working overtime
full force
you see all the parts moving
you move a few inches
the tops not in sight
the horizon is imaginary
it’s in your head
you close your eyes and experience the view
you open your eyes and see nothing
